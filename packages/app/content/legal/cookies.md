## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Cookie Policy explains how Mischief Marmot LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit and use Create Studio at https://create.studio and related services (the "Service").

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. Cookies help websites recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and other settings.

## 3. HOW WE USE COOKIES

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| session_token | User authentication | Session | HTTP-only, Secure |
| csrf_token | Security against cross-site request forgery | Session | HTTP-only, Secure |

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies (Performance)

With your consent, we use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our Service:

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| _ga | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years | Third-party |
| *ga** | Google Analytics | Maintains session state | 2 years | Third-party |
| _gid | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users | 24 hours | Third-party |

## 4. COOKIES WE DO NOT USE

We want to be transparent about what we DON'T collect:

- **Marketing/Advertising Cookies:** We do not use cookies for advertising or remarketing
- **Social Media Cookies:** We do not use social media tracking cookies
- **Profiling Cookies:** We do not create user profiles for targeted advertising
- **Third-Party Tracking:** We do not allow third-party trackers except Google Analytics (with consent)

## 5. YOUR CONSENT

### 5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our Service, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept All:** Enable both essential and analytics cookies
- **Reject Optional:** Only essential cookies will be used
- **Manage Preferences:** Choose which categories of cookies to allow

### 5.2 Consent for Analytics

Google Analytics cookies will only be activated after you provide explicit consent. If you do not consent, we will not track your visit using Google Analytics.

## 6. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

### 6.1 Through Our Service

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

- Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website
- Accessing cookie preferences through your account settings

### 6.2 Through Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to:

- View what cookies are stored
- Delete cookies individually or entirely
- Block third-party cookies
- Block all cookies
- Set preferences for specific websites

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our Service.

### 6.3 Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt-out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

## 7. BROWSER SETTINGS GUIDES

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

## 8. DO NOT TRACK

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. Currently, our Service does not respond to DNT signals. However, you can still control cookie usage through our consent banner and browser settings.

## 9. COOKIES AND PERSONAL DATA

Some cookies may process personal data. For information about how we handle personal data, please see our Privacy Policy. Key points:

- Session cookies are encrypted
- We don't store sensitive data in cookies
- Analytics data is anonymized (no IP addresses)

## 10. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

### 10.1 Google Analytics

Google Analytics places cookies to help us analyze traffic and usage patterns. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 10.2 Cloudflare

As our infrastructure provider, Cloudflare may set technical cookies for security and performance. These are essential for Service operation.

## 11. COOKIES IN EMAILS

We use tracking pixels in transactional emails to:

- Confirm email delivery
- Track if emails are opened
- Improve our email communications

This tracking is minimal and only for transactional emails (password resets, account notifications).

## 12. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be indicated by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice on our Service
- Resetting consent preferences for significant changes

## 13. INTERNATIONAL USERS

### 13.1 EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

We comply with GDPR requirements for cookie consent:

- Explicit consent before non-essential cookies
- Clear information about cookie purposes
- Easy withdrawal of consent
- No cookie walls (access not conditional on accepting cookies)

### 13.2 California Users (CCPA)

California residents have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information through cookies or otherwise.
