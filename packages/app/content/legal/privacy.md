## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Mischief Marmot LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Create, our WordPress plugin and related services (collectively, the "Service"), including the WordPress plugin available at [WordPress repository listing](https://wordpress.org/plugins/mediavine-create) and Create Studio available at https://create.studio.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Service.

## 2. AGE RESTRICTIONS

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use our Service or provide any information to us. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe we might have information from a child under 13, please contact us at [legal@mischiefmarmot.com](mailto:legal@mischiefmarmot.com).

## 3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 3.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**

- Email address
- First and last name
- Password (stored as encrypted hash)
- Site URL and domain information

**Content Information:**

- Recipe content (ingredients, instructions, nutritional data)
- How-to content and instructions
- List content and items
- Schema markup and structured data

**Payment Information:**

- Processed through Stripe
- We do not store credit card numbers
- Stripe receives your email and site ID for payment processing

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Analytics Data:**

- Usage patterns and feature interactions
- Session identifiers (anonymized, no IP addresses)
- Site version and configuration details
- Collected via Google Analytics (ID: G-Q7YTD7XTY0)

**Technical Information:**

- Browser type and version
- Time zone setting
- Operating system
- Platform information

### 3.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service. For detailed information about our cookie usage, please see our Cookie Policy.

**Types of Cookies We Use:**

- **Essential Cookies:** HTTP-only secure session cookies for authentication
- **Analytics Cookies:** Google Analytics cookies (with your consent)

We do not use marketing cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking pixels.

## 5. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers that help us operate our Service:

- **Stripe:** Payment processing (receives email and site ID)
- **Postmark:** Email delivery service (receives email addresses)
- **API Ninjas:** Nutrition calculation (receives recipe ingredients only)
- **Cloudflare:** Infrastructure services (D1 database, KV storage, Blob storage, CDN)
- **Google Analytics:** Website analytics (with your consent)

### 5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, including:

- Court orders or subpoenas
- Government requests
- Legal proceedings

### 5.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any change in ownership or control of your personal information.

### 5.4 Aggregated Information

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information publicly and with partners for business or research purposes.

## 6. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

### 6.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's distributed global network, which may include servers in multiple countries.

### 6.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- Data encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- Session data encrypted at rest
- API keys stored as environment variables
- Regular security audits and updates

### 6.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Service:

- **Session analytics:** 30 days
- **Analytics aggregates:** 1 year
- **Rate limit counters:** 1 year
- **User accounts:** Until deletion requested
- **Payment records:** As required by law (typically 7 years)

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

### 7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal information. Email [legal@mischiefmarmot.com](mailto:legal@mischiefmarmot.com) with subject line "Export Data" including your email address and associated site.

### 7.2 Correction

You may update your account information through your account settings or by contacting us.

### 7.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Email [legal@mischiefmarmot.com](mailto:legal@mischiefmarmot.com) with subject line "Delete Data" including your email address and associated site. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

### 7.4 Opt-Out

- **Analytics:** You can opt-out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
- **Cookies:** You can refuse cookies through your browser settings
- **Emails:** Transactional emails cannot be opted out of while you have an account

### 7.5 Do Not Track

We do not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals.

## 8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

By using our Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your information remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

### 8.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:

- Right to be informed about data processing
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Rights related to automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, contact [legal@mischiefmarmot.com](mailto:legal@mischiefmarmot.com).

### 8.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to equal service and non-discrimination

We do not sell personal information.
