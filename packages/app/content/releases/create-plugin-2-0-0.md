The wait is over. Create 2.0 is a complete reimagining of the Create Plugin — not just new features, but a fundamentally better way to work with structured content.

## Interactive Mode

The headline feature of Create 2.0 is **Interactive Mode** — a first-of-its-kind experience that transforms your recipe cards from static content into living, interactive tools your readers will love.

::releases-release-demo-plugin-200
::

Readers can check off ingredients and steps as they cook, adjust servings with a tap, convert between metric and imperial units, start a built-in timer, and leave a star rating — all without leaving the card.

## Built-In Importers

Switching to Create has never been easier. Enable the importer in Create's settings and bring your recipes home from other plugins with a streamlined, guided experience.

## New Themes & Customization

Create 2.0 ships with beautiful new card themes and gives you more control than ever with custom CSS support directly in settings. Add custom HTML between list items for your ad provider to target.

## Streamlined Workflows

Say goodbye to tedious multi-step processes:

- **Bulk list items** — add multiple ingredients or steps at once
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — work faster with hotkeys
- **Searchable settings** — every setting is illustrative and searchable
- **Collection filters** — find cards quickly with filters and sorting

## What's Next

This is just the beginning. Coming soon: new blocks, new card types, indexes, collections, more themes and layouts, expanded affiliate integrations, Creator Challenges, and much more.
