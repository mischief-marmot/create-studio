Create 2.1 brings a much-requested feature to the [Create WordPress recipe plugin](/releases/create-plugin-2-0-0): full video shortcode support. You can now embed videos from any source — YouTube, Mediavine, Raptive/AdThrive, or your favorite video plugin — directly into your [recipe cards](/features/recipe-cards) and [how-to cards](/features/how-to-cards), with per-card control over where the video appears.

## Video Shortcode Support for Recipe Cards

You can now add videos to your cards using any video shortcode, not just YouTube URLs. Choose from three video types: **YouTube**, **Mediavine**, or **Shortcode** — and paste in the embed code from your preferred video plugin.

::release-demo-plugin-210{feature="video-type"}
::

## Per-Card Video Placement

Control exactly where the video appears on each individual card. Choose to display it above the card content for maximum visibility, or below for a content-first experience. This setting is available per-card, so you can customize it for each piece of content.

## Bug Fixes & Stability

- **Classic Editor stability** — Resolved a crash in TinyMCE when opening the Create card editor
- **Card duplication** — Duplicating a card from a collection view now succeeds reliably
