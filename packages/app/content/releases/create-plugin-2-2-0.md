## Features You Can Unlock

Each of these Pro features becomes available the moment you start your trial. Try one for the first time, and you'll earn a bonus day added to your trial.

### Servings Adjustment

Let your readers scale recipe ingredients up or down with a single click. Whether a recipe serves 4 and your reader needs to cook for 8, quantities update automatically across the entire ingredient list. This is one of the most requested features for WordPress recipe cards — and it works seamlessly with Create's [recipe card](/features/recipe-cards) block.

::release-demo-plugin-220{feature="servings-adjustment"}
::

### Unit Conversion

Readers can toggle between metric and imperial measurements instantly — no mental math required. Food bloggers with international audiences especially benefit from automatic unit conversion, making recipes accessible to readers worldwide.

### Checklists

Add interactive checkboxes to ingredients and instructions so readers can track their progress as they cook or build. Checklists keep readers engaged on the page longer and reduce the frustration of losing their place in a long recipe or [how-to guide](/features/how-to-cards).

::release-demo-plugin-220{feature="checklists"}
::

### Interactive Mode CTAs

Customize how your call-to-action appears on your WordPress recipe cards and how-to cards. Choose from inline banners, floating buttons, and more to drive engagement with your interactive features. Match the CTA style to your blog's design for a seamless reader experience.

### Premium Card Themes

Give your cards a fresh look with premium themes like **Editorial** and **Modern Elegant** — designed to match high-end food and lifestyle blogs. These themes go beyond basic styling, offering polished typography and layout options that make your recipe cards and [roundup lists](/features/lists-and-roundups) stand out.

### Bulk List Importer

Import entire lists of links at once instead of adding them one by one. Paste your URLs and Create scrapes the titles, images, and descriptions automatically — perfect for food bloggers building roundup posts with dozens of recipe links.

::release-demo-plugin-220{feature="bulk-import"}
::

### Review Management

Moderate and respond to reader reviews directly from the WordPress admin. Build engagement and trust with your audience by managing recipe ratings, responding to feedback, and keeping your review sections active and helpful.

::release-demo-plugin-220{feature="review-management"}

## Bug Fixes

Create 2.2 also includes fixes for issues reported by the community:

- **Cache busting** — Caching plugins like WP Super Cache and LiteSpeed Cache could serve stale REST API responses when editing Create cards. The plugin now sends proper cache-control headers to prevent this.
- **List button defaults** — Default list button text now correctly uses the first option from your Button Action Defaults setting instead of a blank value
- **Category & Cuisine display** — Recipe category and cuisine fields in the editor now display human-readable names instead of raw taxonomy IDs
- **Amazon link errors** — Amazon product link scraping now returns specific, actionable error messages instead of generic failures, making it easier to troubleshoot blocked or unavailable links
